Small Beginnings

The EBAC Hiking Club started on the trail. A group of avid hikers getting together, sharing and organizing trips over email. As the group grew, they decided it was time to come together as a club under the EBAC. The group accomplished just that by becoming fully recognized by the EBAC Board in November, 2017. Since then, people from all over have come to share their enthusiam for the trail and fit in a little adventure.


Our Mission

The mission of this EBAC Hiking Club is to be in hiking boots, on the trail as much as possible. We want to bring all those who enjoy hiking and backpacking at Electric Boat together and go places we’ve been and never been before.
